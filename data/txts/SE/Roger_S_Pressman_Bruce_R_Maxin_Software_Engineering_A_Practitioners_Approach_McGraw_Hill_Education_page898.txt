As we move further into the 21st century, software continues to be the most 
important product and the most important industry on the world stage. Its im-
pact and importance have come a long, long way. And yet, a new generation 

of software developers must meet many of the same challenges that faced ear-

lier generations. Let us hope that the people who meet the challenge—software 
engineers—will have the wisdom to develop systems that improve the human 
condition.
